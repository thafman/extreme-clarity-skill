# Changelog

## v0.1 — 2026-04-17

- Initial ship.
- CRITIQUE mode: scores any written asset against the 18-rule rubric, returns top-3 fixes and hard-cap triggers.
- MAKEOVER mode: rewrites to clear 16+/18 while preserving argument and facts; reports before/after score delta.
- Rubric merges Naomi Gleit's 10 doc/meeting-hygiene practices with 8 sentence-level writing rules.
- `install.sh` with `ln -sfn` symlink for macOS + Linux.
